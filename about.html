Her Story

Creative Excellence
in Fashion Design

With over 10 years of experience in fashion design, Sowmiya T brings a rare blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary sensibility to every creation. Her passion for minimal luxury has established Avinya Designers as a trusted name among discerning clients across the country.

Sowmiya's journey started with a single vision: to create clothes that aren't just beautiful, but meaningful. She believes that every piece tells a story, and her designs are crafted to make each wearer feel confident, elegant, and completely authentic.

From her first national fashion show in 2015 to designing complete bridal trousseaux, every milestone has been shaped by one belief — that great design is deeply personal.
